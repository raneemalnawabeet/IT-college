About the college :

KASIT was established in year 2000, with the directives of His Majesty King Abdullah II in the development of IT in Jordan. Proceeding from the goals and aspirations of (KASIT) in the preparation of scientific cadres specializing in the field of IT; to supply the local and Arab market, the faculty has worked on providing programs and laboratories equipped to achieve its vision.

The College got accreditation of ABET

The accreditation of ABET is to ensure that the section obtained applies the quality standards of education agreed upon by the relevant experience in education to equip and qualify students. For example, the specifications of education in engineering programs are developed by experts in engineering education and specifications of education in computer programs are developed by experts in this field ..... and so on. The development of specifications by the competent people gives them the opportunity to communicate with the educational community to cooperate in the drafting and amendment of these specifications and ensure their proper application.
